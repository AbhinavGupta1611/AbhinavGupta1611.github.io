About Me

I am a Hydrologist with expertise in mathematical and statistical modeling, specializing in water quality and hydrological processes. My research focuses on large-scale hydrological modeling and decision-making under uncertainty, combining physics-based models with machine learning and deep learning techniques.

I integrate hydro-meteorological data, remote sensing information, and water quality measurements to improve predictions and inform sustainable water resource management strategies.
