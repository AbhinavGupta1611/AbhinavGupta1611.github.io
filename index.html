About Me

I am a Hydrologist trained in mathematical and statistical modeling. My research focuses on computer simulations of watershed- to regional-scale hydrological and water quality processes, with a special focus on Decision Making Under Deep Uncertainty. I develop physics-based and machine/deep learning (ML/DL) models to integrate hydro-meteorological, remote sensing, and water quality data.
